About Me

Passionate about technology, I am transitioning from a successful career in sound production to full-stack web development. Graduated with Bcomm Strategic Management, I am currently immersed in an intensive JavaScript and MERN Stack bootcamp with Stellenbosch University and HyperionDev. I am committed to mastering modern web technologies while documenting my progress through a 'Building in Public' approach.

My previous experience in audio engineering has ingrained in me a meticulous attention to detail and a relentless work ethic, qualities that now drive my programming journey. Alongside the bootcamp, I am actively deepening my knowledge through The Odin Project’s JavaScript and Node.js path, ensuring a robust understanding of key concepts as I advance.

I am particularly motivated by challenging projects that push the boundaries of my knowledge, and I thrive in environments that foster continuous learning and innovation. My goal is to leverage my technical and creative skills in a dynamic role as a Junior Web Developer, where I can contribute meaningfully to the advancement of technology.
